What do I get?
We compiled detailed information on leading art collectors around the world. Profile’s completeness can vary depending on the information available. It can include contact details, information on artists in collection, type of art engagements and personal information.

Yes. We only compile information that is also publicly available on accessible sources such as magazines, internet etc.
For how long can I access the profile?
You can access it for one year.
How long are my credits valid?
Credits don’t have an expiry date. You can use them anytime and also top-up your credits anytime.
